# Support — Task Party

_Last updated: 2026-05-25_

Task Party is a study and task-tracking app for students. If you need help, have a bug to report, or want to make a privacy request, you're in the right place.

## Contact

The fastest way to reach us is by email:

**conventoc@g.ucla.edu**

Please include:

- Your device model and iOS version (e.g. _iPhone 14, iOS 18.2_)
- The email address you use to sign in to Task Party
- A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened
- A screenshot, if applicable

We aim to reply within 3 business days.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap **Sign in with email**, enter your email address, then tap **Forgot password?**. Firebase will send a password reset link to your inbox. If you originally signed in with Apple or Google, use that same provider — there is no separate password to reset.

### How do I delete my account?

Email **conventoc@g.ucla.edu** with the subject line `Account deletion` and the email address you signed up with. We will delete your account and associated data within 30 days and confirm by email. See the [Privacy Policy](./) for full details on what is deleted.

### My tasks aren't syncing between devices

1. Confirm you are signed in with the same account on both devices (Profile → check the email shown).
2. Check your internet connection — Task Party syncs through Firebase and needs an active connection to push changes.
3. Force-quit and reopen the app to trigger a fresh sync.

If tasks still don't appear, email support with the approximate time you created the missing task and we'll investigate.

### Why does Task Party include a Blackjack mini-game?

The Blackjack screen is an in-app mini-game played with **earned in-app coins only**. There are no real-money transactions, no gambling for prizes, and no way to convert coins to real currency. It's a short break between study sessions.

### Is Task Party free?

Yes. Task Party is free to use. There are currently no subscriptions or in-app purchases.

### How is my data used?

Your tasks, schedule, and account info are stored in Firebase so they sync across your devices. We do not sell your data and do not use it for advertising. Full details: [Privacy Policy](./).

### I found a bug / I have a feature request

Send it to **conventoc@g.ucla.edu** with the subject line `Bug` or `Feature request`. Specific reproduction steps and screenshots are extremely helpful.
